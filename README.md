## What is Gabriola Connects?

Gabriola Connects is a free, community-driven platform built by islanders, for islanders. We created this website to help our community of 4,500 residents stay connected, informed, and engaged with what's happening on Gabriola Island.

**Website:** [gabriolaconnects.ca](https://gabriolaconnects.ca)

---

## Why We Built This

Living on Gabriola Island is wonderful, but staying informed about community events, local services, and island news can be challenging. Information gets scattered across different websites, social media groups, bulletin boards, and word-of-mouth.

We experienced this firsthand and decided to do something about it. **Gabriola Connects' goal is to bring everything together** in one easy-to-use place, making it simple to:

- **Discover** what's happening on the island
- **Connect** with your neighbors
- **Support** local businesses
- **Stay informed** about ferry schedules and community alerts

### üîî Community Alerts

**Stay informed - on your terms.**

Our alert system is designed to help you be as informed (or as unplugged) as you want to be. You control what you see and when you see it.

- **Emergency alerts** - Critical information about safety, evacuations, or urgent community needs
- **Community announcements** - Important updates about island services, road closures, or public meetings
- **Event reminders** - Optional notifications about upcoming activities (if you choose to enable them)

**Your choice, always.** We believe you should decide how much information you want to receive. Whether you check alerts daily or only during emergencies, the system works the way you need it to.

Set your preferences to match your lifestyle - from "tell me everything" to "emergencies only" to "I'll check when I want to."

## Privacy & Security: What You Should Know

We believe in transparency about how your data is handled and protected. Here's what you need to know in plain language:

### üîí Private Messages Are Truly Private

**Your private messages are exactly that - private.** When you send a private message to another user:

- ‚úÖ Only you and the recipient can read it
- ‚úÖ Messages are stored encrypted in the database
- ‚úÖ **Admins and moderators CANNOT read your private messages**
- ‚úÖ We (the Strachan family) cannot read your private messages
- ‚úÖ Messages cannot be intercepted or viewed by anyone except sender and recipient

**Exception:** If you report harassment or abuse through private messages, you may be asked to provide screenshots as evidence. We cannot access the messages ourselves to investigate.

### üëÄ What Admins and Moderators CAN See

**Public Forum Posts:** Moderators can see, edit, or remove forum posts and comments to maintain community standards.

**User Profiles:** Basic information like your name, profile photo, and bio are visible to moderators.

**Activity Patterns:** Admins can see general activity (number of posts, login times) to help maintain platform health.

**What They CANNOT See:** Your private messages, your password (encrypted), your private contact information beyond what you choose to share publicly.

### üõ°Ô∏è How Your Data is Protected

**Passwords:** Your password is encrypted using industry-standard methods. Even we can't see what your password is.

**Data Storage:** All data is stored securely on Supabase (a trusted platform used by thousands of companies). We don't store data on our personal computers.

**No Data Selling:** We will never sell, share, or give away your data to third parties. Period.

**No Tracking:** We don't use tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or analytics that follow you around the internet.

**Data Deletion:** If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our database.

### üìä What We Collect and Why

**Essential Information:**
- Email address (to create your account and contact you if needed)
- Name (so neighbors know who they're talking to)
- Password (encrypted, so you can log in securely)

**Optional Information:**
- Profile photo (to personalize your presence)
- Bio and location (to help connect with neighbors)
- Phone number (only if you want to be contacted this way)

**Automatically Collected:**
- Posts and comments you create (they're meant to be shared!)
- Events you create or RSVP to
- Login times (to help us troubleshoot issues)

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Your browsing history on other websites
- Your location data (unless you manually enter it)
- Your contacts or address book
- Credit card information (the platform is free!)

### üîì Open Source Transparency

The entire Gabriola Connects codebase is **open source** on GitHub. This means:

- ‚úÖ Anyone can review the code to verify our privacy claims
- ‚úÖ Security researchers can identify and report vulnerabilities
- ‚úÖ The community can see exactly how data is handled
- ‚úÖ There are no secret backdoors or hidden tracking

**For the technically curious:** Visit our GitHub repository to see exactly how we handle data, encryption, and privacy.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è What You Should Know

**Email Communications:** We may occasionally email you about important platform updates or security issues. You can unsubscribe from non-essential emails.

**Public Posts Are Public:** Anything you post in the forum or calendar is visible to all users. Don't share personal information you want to keep private in public forums.

**Deleted Content:** When you delete a forum post, it's removed from public view but may remain in backups for a short period for technical reasons.

**Beta Stage:** As we're in beta, security is continuously being reviewed and improved. If you discover a security issue, please report it to us immediately.

### ü§ù Our Commitment

We promise to:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it really free?
Yes! No subscription fees, no hidden costs, no ads. Gabriola Connects is provided as a service to our community.

### Do I have to live on Gabriola to use it?
Anyone can view the calendar, directory, ferry schedules, and community alerts. Creating an account and participating in the forum is open to everyone, though we verify island residents for certain features.

### How do you make money if it's free?
We don't. This is a community service project, not a business. The platform costs are covered by the Strachan family as their contribution to the island community.

### Can businesses advertise here?
Local businesses can list in our free directory. We will never have paid advertising - we want to keep the platform focused on community, not commerce.

### What about my privacy?
We collect only what's necessary to run the platform (email, name, etc.). We don't sell data, share information with third parties, or track you across the internet. Your data stays with us and is used only to provide you with community features.

### Who moderates the forum?
Volunteer community members who care about maintaining a respectful, helpful space. Moderation is done transparently with clear community guidelines.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, anytime. You're in control of your data.

### Will there be a mobile app?
Yes! A mobile app is in our development roadmap. For now, the website works great on mobile browsers.
